# Golden Prompt: Phased Single Take

## Source Brief

A continuous take of a courier noticing a signal light.

## Internal Prompt Specification

Shot structure: phased_single_take. Use Beginning / Then / Finally. No shot labels or hard cuts.

## Compiled Natural-Language Prompt

Beginning: the courier stands in the wet rooftop doorway, breathing steady, holding the closed metal case. Then: a red signal light reflects across the puddle and she turns her eyes toward it without stepping away. Finally: she grips the case tighter and stops in the doorway, the red reflection pulsing softly on the metal. One continuous camera hold, no cuts.

lint: pass
